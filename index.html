Salahuddin Ayubi: The Unifier of the Muslim World

About Salahuddin Ayubi

Salahuddin Ayubi, also known as Saladin, was a Kurdish Muslim leader who united the Muslim world and led the fight against the Crusaders.
He is remembered for his bravery, wisdom, and chivalry, and is considered one of the greatest heroes of Islam. Born in 1137 CE in Tikrit, Iraq,
Salahuddin Ayubi rose to power as the Sultan of Egypt and Syria, and is famous for recapturing Jerusalem from the Crusaders in 1187 CE. His
leadership and military prowess earned him a lasting legacy in Islamic history.

Major Accomplishments

  1. Recaptured Jerusalem from the Crusaders (1187 CE)
  2. United the Muslim world under a single leadership
  3. Defeated the Crusaders at the Battle of Hattin (1187 CE)
